Raspberry Oatmeal Cookie Bars

Description

This oatmeal cookie is one of my favorite cookie bar recipes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • ½ cup packed light brown sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon baking soda
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup butter, softened
  • ¾ cup seedless raspberry jam
